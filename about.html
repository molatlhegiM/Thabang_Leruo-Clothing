About Us

About Thabang Leruo Clothing & Manufacturing

Founded in 2023, Thabang Leruo Clothing & Manufacturing proudly provides premium-quality clothing solutions across South Africa. We specialize in crafting golf shirts, sports kits, school uniforms, and custom sublimated apparel for clubs, corporations, universities, and preschools nationwide.

Our commitment to innovation and excellence has quickly positioned us as a trusted name in the clothing and uniform manufacturing industry.

Our Specialties

  • Golf Shirts and Sport Kits
  • School Uniforms (Pre-School, Primary, and High School)
  • Custom Sublimated Apparel
  • Corporate and Club Uniforms

We utilize advanced sublimation technology — a process where heat transfers ink onto fabric. This ensures vibrant, durable, and fade-resistant designs with unlimited color possibilities, making Pulse Clothing the ideal choice for your apparel needs.
